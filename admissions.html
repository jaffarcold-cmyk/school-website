Admission Criteria

To apply for admission, all candidates must meet the following requirements and provide the necessary documents at the time of application.

  • 8th & 10th Class Marksheet

    Official 8th-grade marksheet for admission in 9th class and 10th-grade marksheet for admission in 11th class.

  • Aadhar Card

    A photocopy of your Aadhar card is required for identification and address verification.

  • Ladakh Residence Certificate

    A valid residence certificate issued by the competent authority in Ladakh.

  • Passport-sized Photograph

    One recent color photograph (3.5 cm x 4.5 cm) with a white background in JPEG format.
