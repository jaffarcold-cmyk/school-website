OUR HISTORY

A Legacy Since 1987

Government Girls Higher Secondary School, Kargil, was founded in 1987 as a modest institution dedicated to girls’ education. Over the years, we have grown into a beacon of empowerment and academic excellence, playing a pivotal role in transforming lives and advancing educational opportunities in Kargil.

We stand as a beacon of educational excellence in the Ladakh region. Our institution is dedicated to empowering young girls through quality education, fostering a learning environment that nurtures intellectual growth, leadership, and ethical responsibility.

Our Mission

To empower and inspire the young women of Kargil by providing equitable access to high-quality education in a safe, inclusive, and supportive environment.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of excellence in girls’ education, transforming lives and communities by nurturing future leaders, change-makers, and compassionate global citizens.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Resiliance and Compassion guide every aspect of our educational methods and community interactions.
