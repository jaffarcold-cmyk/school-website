Academic Streams

We offer comprehensive programs for classes 9 to 12, focusing on holistic development and academic rigor.

Science Stream

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, and Computer Science. Equipped with modern laboratories for practical experimentation.

Commerce Stream

Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Entrepreneurship. Preparing students for the modern financial world.

Humanities Stream

History, Political Science, Geography, Sociology, and Languages. Encouraging critical thinking and social awareness.
